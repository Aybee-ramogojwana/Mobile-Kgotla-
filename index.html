About Mobile Kgotla

Our Mission

We are a citizen team working with communities across Botswana experiencing economic knowledge gaps to reimagine, rebalance, and transform the economy. Our mission is to democratize access to economic data and make it understandable for everyone.

Mobile Kgotla's mission of indigenous innovation, enterprise connectivity, and community empowerment is inspired by the kgotla principle of "mafoko a kgotla a mantle otlhe" (words from the kgotla are all valuable).

We are committed to enabling inclusive economic activity, with focus on vulnerable SMEs, transformational infrastructure, empowerment transactions, and farming ventures led by mostly rural people.

Our Vision

We envision a Botswana where economic data is accessible, understandable, and actionable for all citizens, regardless of their technical expertise or background. By bridging economic knowledge gaps, we aim to empower communities to make informed decisions about their economic future.

We hope to see Mobile Kgotla grow regionally and eventually globally. Each country needs a standard for citizen participation-related data for its internal interoperability and external credibility.

Our goal is to transform how communities engage with economic information, shifting from passive consumers to active participants in shaping economic narratives and policies.

Key Features

Mobile Kgotla (CED) offers a range of innovative features designed to make economic data accessible, understandable, and actionable for all citizens.

Community Integration

Interfaces community development efforts with key stakeholder projects for synergic development.

Simplified Economic Terms

Breaks down technical economic terms in a way non-specialists can understand.

Local Focus

Designed with a local focus, breaking down data by borough and allowing easy comparisons between different local areas across Botswana's 31 local authorities.

Contextualized Data

Contextualizes data by explaining what the data shows and its limitations and biases, providing a complete picture for better decision-making.

Spatial Economy Maps

Creates bridges between enterprise activities to produce spatial economy maps showing key areas for improvement through heat maps and visual data representation.

Trend Analysis

Produces trend analysis and expert insights on locality economy performance to feed global development narratives and inform local decision-making processes.
